About Us


Herbal Hub:

"Herbal Hub" a groundbreaking online platform dedicated to tackling food waste by connecting surplus food resources with orphanages and animal husbandries in need. Our mission is to bridge the gap between excess food and those who can benefit from it the most, simultaneously reducing wastage and contributing to the well-being of vulnerable populations. The platform utilizes geolocation services to identify surplus food donors and recipients in close proximity, ensuring that food donations can be delivered efficiently and without unnecessary transportation costs. Donors can easily update their surplus food inventory on the platform, including details such as food type, quantity, expiration dates, and pickup availability. This helps streamline the donation process.

Explore our carefully curated selection of high-quality herbal products, including dried herbs, herbal teas, tinctures, essential oils, and more. We collaborate with trusted suppliers to ensure you have access to the best herbal products available.
